ABOUT
LIVE LOCAL MUSIC
Patches Pub & Grill is your go-to destination for live local music, creating an energetic and vibrant atmosphere for all. Join us as we showcase talented artists and bands, ensuring that every night is filled with great tunes and unforgettable memories. With our welcoming environment and dedicated staff, we guarantee an experience that keeps you coming back for more.
AWARD WINNING FOOD
At Patches Pub & Grill, we pride ourselves on our award-winning food that caters to every palate. From mouth-watering burgers to fresh seafood dishes, our menu is designed to please. Each meal is crafted with high-quality ingredients, ensuring that every bite is packed with flavor. Whether you're here for a casual meal or a special occasion, our culinary delights will leave you satisfied.
PARTY @ PATCHES
Patches does not rest; there's always something happening! From Turkey Bowling to Hippie Parties, there's never a dull moment. Enjoy our lively Happy Hours, Craft Beer Hours, Comedy Shows, Pub Crawls, and Boat Trips. You just never know what the owners of Patches Pub & Grill will come up with next, but you can be sure it's going to be a blast!
